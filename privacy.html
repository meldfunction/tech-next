Tech Next Program

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Information We Collect

When you register for a Tech Next workshop or orientation, we collect your name, email address, company name, and any information you voluntarily provide in registration forms. The Federal Readiness Diagnostic tool runs entirely in your browser — no responses are transmitted to or stored on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

We use registration information solely to communicate event details, send reminders, and follow up with resources relevant to the workshop. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Retention

Registration data is retained for up to 12 months following an event to allow for follow-up resource sharing. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to the Tech Next program team via the contact page.
